About Me

Hello! I'm Sunny Yarramsetti, completed my M.Sc. in Data Analytics at the Technological University of the Shannon (TUS), Limerick. I'm passionate about transforming raw data into actionable insights that help drive decision-making and business growth.

My academic journey builds on a B.Tech in Computer Science from Parul University, where I developed strong foundations in programming, databases, and problem-solving. Over the years, I've gained hands-on experience with machine learning, forecasting, and data visualization, supported by real-world projects in electricity demand forecasting and polymer solubility prediction.

Professionally, I worked as a Frontend Developer Intern at Quantum Wave, where I built a React application for Railway Fire Extinguisher Services, digitizing safety tracking and reducing manual reporting effort by ~40%. I have strong skills across Python, SQL, Java, Power BI, Tableau, and MySQL and experience using frameworks like LightGBM, ARIMA, XGBoost, and LSTM.

I'm motivated to apply my skills in real-world, dynamic environments, contribute to impactful projects, and continuously grow as a data professional.
